A Sacred Pause

Before you begin writing,
take a moment to arrive.

Close the extra tabs. Set your phone aside. If it feels right, light a candle, pour something warm, or simply take three slow breaths.

This form is not a task to rush through. It is the opening of our work together. The way you answer these questions — honestly, without editing yourself — is how I begin to understand your business, your vision, and where we need to go.

There is no right way to do this. There is only your way.

Choose the tool that feels truest

✎
Write by hand first Let your pen move before your editor does. Write your answers on paper, then come back and type them here when you're ready.
⌨
Type directly If the keyboard is where your thoughts flow clearest, start here. Let it be a stream — you can always refine later.
◘
Speak your answers Sometimes the most powerful way to find your truth is to say it out loud. Use your phone's voice-to-text and let your words land unfiltered. You might surprise yourself.
