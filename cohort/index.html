The Five Weeks

Each week builds on the last.

Week 01 · Identity

Your Brand Identity Statement.

You work through your Brand Attributes and Brand Identity Statement, guided by Kiana and the CORE framework. You name who you are, in your own words. The foundation everything else builds on.

Week 02 · Audience

Your Ideal Client Avatars.

You develop real, specific, named profiles of the people your work is for. No abstractions. No demographics-only sketches. The exact humans your brand is speaking to, on the page.

Week 03 · Direction

Your Strategic Direction.

Priorities clarified. Where to focus the time you have. Which moves matter, which can wait. The map of your next chapter, on one page.

Week 04 · Aesthetic

Three Creative Directions.

You generate three Creative Directions with AI from the stacked foundation. One picks itself. The visual register your brand actually lives in, sourced from the strategy underneath.

Week 05 · Integration

Your Coherent Brand AI™.

Everything comes together. You install your Coherent Brand AI into your own AI workspace. You ship a demonstrable product built on your now-coherent foundation. The work closes with ceremony.

What you walk away with

A complete Brand Operating System.

  • Your Brand Identity Statement, your Ideal Client Avatars, your Strategic Direction
  • Three Creative Directions, one picked and ready to build from
  • Your own Coherent Brand AI, installable into your AI workspace
  • A complete foundation your team and your AI tools both work from
