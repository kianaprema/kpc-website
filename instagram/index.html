3 Things to Get Right Before You Hire a Brand Designer
1 / 5
3 Things to Get Right
Before You Hire
a Brand Designer
Kiana Prema Consulting
2 / 5
1
Know who you're for.
Not "everyone who needs what I sell." Your actual ideal human. Their fears, desires, and the language they use.
3 / 5
2
Know what you stand for.
Your point of view, your non-negotiables, the thing that makes you different from the 47 other people who do something similar.
4 / 5
3
Know where you're going.
Not just next quarter. Where do you want to be in 3 years? Your brand needs to be built for that version of your business.
5 / 5
If you don't have these
three things dialed...
You don't need a designer yet.
You need a strategist.
